Why Readmissa

Built on zero manual input.
By design.

Every competitor asks someone to do something extra. Readmissa is built on the principle that the data flows automatically from patient to clinician, frictionlessly.

Zero manual data entry
The AI extracts clinical data from natural conversation. Patients talk; Readmissa structures. No ticking boxes, no survey fatigue.
Risk scoring updated daily
Each patient gets a score recalculated after every check-in. The most at-risk always surface to the top, no manual sorting required.
24/7 AI companion
Not a chatbot. A clinically-aware companion that knows the patient's condition, discharge plan, and when to escalate, around the clock.
NHS FHIR-ready architecture
Built to integrate with NHS systems from day one. FHIR compliance isn't a roadmap item, it's foundational to the architecture.
Both sides of the discharge
Patient app and clinician dashboard in a single platform. No integration required, the data loop is built in from the ground up.
Software-only deployment
No hardware. No wearables required. Lower deployment friction means faster NHS adoption and cleaner private hospital rollouts.
